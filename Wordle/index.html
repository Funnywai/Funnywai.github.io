Wordle Remix

Guess the five letter word

Six tries. Colors tell you how close you are: green is correct, gold is present, slate is absent.

How to play

  • Type with your keyboard or tap the on-screen keys.
  • Press Enter to submit. Backspace deletes.
  • Each letter can appear more than once. Use the colors to narrow it down.
  • Hit New Game anytime for a fresh word.
